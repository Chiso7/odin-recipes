Carbonara

Description

This is a delicious plate of pasta made by the italians back in the 1800.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Eggs
  • Guaniciale
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the water for several minutes
  2. Add the pasta when the water starts boiling
  3. Add the eggs as well
  4. Take the pasta out and season it
